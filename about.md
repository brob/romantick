### **jeffrey bowman**

jeffrey bowman is a multimedia artist and druid living and working in metro detroit, mi. working primarily in oils, his focus is exploring spiritualism through abstract paintings. the music he creates is of the same focus: spiritualism. often using a variety of instruments including several gongs he tries to create a sacred atmosphere for his audience. you can follow his adventures at [bowmanism.com](http://www.bowmanism.com).
